IRON SLIDING GATE

  • An Iron sliding gate is a type of gate that opens horizontally by sliding, usually parallel to a wall. Sliding gates can be mounted either on top of a track below or be suspended from a track above and some types 'disappear' in a wall when slid open.

  • Motorised Iron Sliding Gate

    Motorised Gates are not only creatively attractive but vigorous and faultlessly aligned. The pre-tensioning of the motorized gate leaf construction decreases the danger of bending. Electromechanically driven remote-controlled steel gates are hard and apt to be installed at any industrial, residential, or commercial premises. The automatic gates safeguard long term steadiness and exceptionally blends in with the style of the buildings.

  • Manual Iron Sliding Gate

    We provide superior grade conglomerate of Manual or Non-Automatic Gates, which is manufactured using premium-grade steel. These gates have a protective covering that protects them from the harmful effects of chemicals. Apart from this, we offer these to the clients in various color combinations, designs, and laminates.

    We also provide full design casting gates that are attractive and specially designed for entrance gates.
