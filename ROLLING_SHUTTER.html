ROLLING SHUTTER

  • A rolling shutter is a type of door or window shutter consisting of many horizontal slats (or sometimes bars or web systems) hinged together. The door is raised to open it and lowered to close it. It provides protection against wind, rain, fire, and theft. In shutter form, it is used in front of a window and protects the window from vandalism and burglary attempts.

  • Motorised Rolling Shutter

    The most popular and widely used amongst all the categories of rolling shutter is the motorized rolling shutter. The symbolic feature of motorized rolling shutters is its robust structure obtained due to the use of high strength metals in the making of slate and guide profile. If on one hand, these shutters require low maintenance enabling very less inspection & manual attention then, on the other hand, our motorized rolling shutters have very easy operation ensuring total customer satisfaction.

    (i) Perforated Motorised Rolling Shutter
    (ii) Grill Motorised Rolling Shutter
    (iii) Round Profile Motorised Rolling Shutter
    (iv) Flat Profile Motorised Rolling Shutter
    (v) Aircraft Hanger Motorised Rolling Shutter

  • CHAIN PULLE ROLLING SHUTTER

    In this, a manual chain pulley is being used for rolling the shutter horizontally/vertically. The chains are of stainless steel, for which it requires low maintenance.

    In this, a gear drive from the shutter roller traced through the building facade to a universal joint on the room side that is operated by a cranked winding handle.
