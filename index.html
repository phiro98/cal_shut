ABOUT US

Calcutta Rolling Shutter and Shed Engineering Works is a well-growing engineering organisation established in 2016. We are engaged in manufacturing and supplying a diverse range of Rolling Shutters, Sliding Swinging Gates, Parking Barriers, Roofing Sheet, Roadway Safety Products Building Construction Material. These products are highly appreciated for their durability, sturdy construction, dimensional accuracy, low maintenance and easy installation. Our products are a part of various commercial and non-commercial establishments across the country.
